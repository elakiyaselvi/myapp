About Us

Pastor Newton O. Denila is the General overseer of Grace For All Ministries, House of Favour. An Electronic/Telecoms Engineer by training, his work experience includes many years in a managerial position at a multinational Organisation as well as over two decades as Senior Pastor thereafter, during which he has made a steady and purposeful advancement through the ladder of spiritual leadership.

Pastor Newton, a teacher of the Word with an uncommon spiritual and biblical insight is sharp-witted. He is blessed with a prophetic unction and combines rare spiritual qualities with a special calling to the body of Christ (The Church). He believes that we are in an age where the building of a spiritual and glorious people through the teaching of the Word and deliverance is paramount. A thorough and inspiring leader who has preached the word at various meetings in Nigeria and Overseas. He also holds an MBA in International Business.
